Pantry Mouse

Pantry Mouse is a home inventory management app built with Ionic, enabling users to efficiently track pantry items. By leveraging a barcode scanning feature, users can scan UPC codes with their phone's camera, retrieving product data through a third-party API. This data is processed and stored in a local SQLite database, allowing users to add items, update quantities, and monitor stock levels. The app features color-coded quantity indicators to signal when items need replenishing. Future enhancements include automated shopping list generation and one-touch integration with online retailers for seamless grocery replenishment.
